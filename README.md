# 🏥 Sanjeevani Medical Report Portal

A smart healthcare platform developed during **TechBhasha HackFest 2.0** by **Team Titans** under the **Health & Fitness** domain.

## 📌 Problem Statement

Managing medical records across different hospitals is difficult. Patients often lose reports, appointment booking is time-consuming, and doctors cannot easily access previous medical history.

## 💡 Proposed Solution

**Sanjeevani Medical Report Portal** is a centralized healthcare platform that enables patients and doctors to securely manage medical records, book appointments, upload/download reports, and communicate through a single digital portal.

## ✨ Key Features

* 🔐 Secure Email OTP Authentication
* 👨‍⚕️ Doctor Portal
* 📅 Online Appointment Booking
* 📂 Medical Report Upload
* 📥 Download & Share Reports
* 💊 Medicine Reminder
* 🎥 Video Consultation
* 🚨 Emergency Medical Card
* 📊 Patient Dashboard
* 📄 E-Prescription Support
